01  /  The Problem

Most drone program delays aren’t mechanical.

Parameters get tweaked between flights, never reviewed, never rolled back. Mission day comes and the aircraft fails its preflight — or worse, doesn't get one.

Tests don't repeat

Manual checklists drift. The flight that passed last week fails today and nobody knows what changed.

Field and cloud are split

Field teams log to spreadsheets. HQ has no view. Nothing lines up when something goes wrong.
